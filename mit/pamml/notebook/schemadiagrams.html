Describing Permissions

Permissions in PAMML are similar to Unix. The definition and creation of users, and groups are handled outside of the language. 'Other' refers to those users that are neither the user that created the model or a user that is part of a specified group. Here are the PermissionType definitions:

  • read: the model may be read
  • write: the model may be changed
  • execute: the model may be run/executed
  • add alternatives: alternative models may be specified for this one
