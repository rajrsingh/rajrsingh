PAMML Model Design

Example problem: Modeling population density

Task Outline

  • Acquire Census block group data
  • Identify area and total population variables
  • Calculate population density (area / total population)
  • Aggregate density measurements into quintiles (5 data groupings with an equal number of members in each)
